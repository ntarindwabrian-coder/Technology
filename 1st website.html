Our Story

Powering Rwanda's Digital Future

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Kigali, NEXUS TECH began as a small passion project — two engineers who believed Rwanda deserved world-class technology at fair prices.

Today, we serve over 12,000 customers, partner with 30+ global brands, and operate from our flagship Kigali Innovation City store plus nationwide delivery. Our mission: make premium technology accessible to every innovator, student, and professional in Africa.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery outside Kigali?
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Rwanda via trusted courier partners. Delivery to Kigali typically takes 24 hours, and other provinces 2–3 business days. Shipping fees vary by location and order weight.
Are all products under warranty?
Absolutely. All products come with the manufacturer's original warranty. We also offer our NEXUS Protection Plan for extended coverage, covering accidental damage, liquid damage, and hardware failure.
Can I pay via Mobile Money?
Yes! We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa/Mastercard (debit and credit), bank transfers, and cash in-store. Installment plans are available for orders above 300,000 RWF.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with packaging. Once we receive the returned item, a full refund is processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.
Do you have corporate / bulk pricing?
Yes! We offer special pricing for businesses, schools, NGOs, and government institutions. Contact our sales team at bulk@nexustech.rw or fill out the inquiry form above and mention "bulk order" in your message.
